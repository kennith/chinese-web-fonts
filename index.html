Chinese Fonts

In this page, everything is reset from the Bootstrap framework. While it looks good on English, it does not necessary look on other languages.

The Chinese characters have more strokes than English letters. The Bootstrap default the font size is 1rem, in my opinion, it is easier to read a Chinese characters by changing it to 1.5rem.

It is also better to add more spacing between the Chinese characters. I added 0.1rem between characters.
